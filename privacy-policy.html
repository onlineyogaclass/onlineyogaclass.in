Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Onlineyogaclass.in, we recognize that your health journey is deeply personal. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, protect, and handle your information across our website and clinical programs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better clinical outcomes and a personalized experience. This includes:

  • Personal Identity: Name, age, and contact details (email, WhatsApp number).
  • Health Data: Information you voluntarily provide regarding PCOS, fertility history, thyroid levels, or other medical conditions during consultations.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details required for billing (processed via secure third-party gateways).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data via cookies to improve site performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for clinical and operational purposes:

  • To design personalized yoga protocols tailored to your specific health needs.
  • To communicate session timings, demo bookings, and program updates.
  • To share research-backed wellness guides (PDFs) that you have requested.
  • To analyze the effectiveness of our clinical protocols (anonymized for research purposes).

3. Data Protection & Confidentiality

As a Research Scholar-led practice, we treat your health data with the highest degree of confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Access to health history is restricted to Shringarika Mishra and her core clinical team under strict non-disclosure protocols.

4. International Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

For our clients in the USA, UK, Canada, and EU, we acknowledge your rights to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your personal records ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party tools to enhance our service:

  • Google Forms: For initial health assessments and PDF guide requests.
  • WhatsApp: For real-time clinical communication and support.
  • Swiper.js/CDN: To provide a smooth visual experience on our website.

6. Cookies

Our website uses "cookies" to enhance user experience. You can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the site may not function properly as a result.
